Tournament Performance Calculator

Calculate player performance ratings from tournament results

This page uses ab initio global optimization — the approach behind FargoRate — to find the single set of ratings for all players that best explains the observed match results. The resulting ratings capture each player's snapshot performance, good day or bad day, across the tournaments loaded here.

Help

Tournament URLs
Paste a DigitalPool tournament URL to load match data. Click + to add more tournaments and jointly optimize all players across them. Click − to remove a tournament.
Shareable links
The URL bar updates automatically as you add tournaments, so you can bookmark or share a direct link.
Column sorting
Click any column header to sort by that column. Click again to reverse the sort order.
Official Rating
Editable. Type a new value to override a player's official rating and re-run the optimization. Useful when a player's Fargo rating is missing or outdated.
Perf Rating
The computed performance rating for each player, based on how the entire tournament unfolded. Higher means stronger performance.
Delta
The difference between the performance rating and the official rating. Green positive values mean the player performed above their official rating; red negative values mean below.
Match details
Click a player's name to expand their row and see individual match results. When multiple tournaments are loaded, matches are grouped by tournament.
